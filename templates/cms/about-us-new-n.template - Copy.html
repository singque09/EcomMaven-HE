Beautiful. Luxurious. Personal

At The Hamper Emporium, we strive for nothing short of excellence. Our mission is to connect people through luxury gifting. Gone are the days of tacky baskets wrapped in cellophane. You won’t find tired, mundane grocery store products in our hampers. Instead, make way for world-class wines, gourmet treats, and gorgeous pamper gifts. Each hamper is curated with the utmost care to make your gesture unforgettable.

Join us & come along for the ride
We believe...
1

Your recipient Deserves luxury

Regardless of whether you’re gifting for a special occasion or ‘just because’, they deserve the very best. Our expert team has designed each hamper to be a luxurious experience.

When you’re gifting, don’t settle for second-rate. Add a splash of indulgence to their everyday life with a present that exceeds their expectations.

2

Supporting local Benefits the community

We are proud to partner with incredible Australian brands and producers. For us, it’s a win-win situation. Our hamper recipients are able to sample new products they may not have tried otherwise, which enriches the gifting experience. We also love helping smaller brands grow by increasing the scope of their incredible products.

3

Quality ingredients Make quality gifts

It’s true, we have high standards. That means we source only the finest items to be included in our gift hampers. Why? Because every detail counts when you’re curating the perfect gift. With over a decade of experience, we have learnt that quality begets quality. We put time and effort into ensuring your gift is exceptional.

4

Hampers connect People

By selecting the perfect hamper for your recipient, you’re showing them your appreciation and care. Take advantage of our free premium gift card to write them a personalised message that they will cherish. Regardless whether those who matter to you are nearby or far apart, the act of gifting will always bring you closer together.

5

Gifting should Be Uncomplicated

Whoever said giving high-quality gifts needs to be difficult? We seek to add simplicity to your gifting process. Because first impressions count, we present our hampers in luxury gift boxes that are bound to impress. No further wrapping is required! Our completely free shipping Australia-wide means distance is no obstacle to showing how much you care.

6

Your brand can make a lasting impact

You’re at the forefront of your industry. Why would you trust your reputation to someone who isn’t at the forefront of theirs? As experts in corporate gift hampers, we can help your business stay top of mind all year long with corporate gifting.

Impress your colleagues and clients with our extensive range and corporate branding options.

The people who make it happen

Founded in 2007, The Hamper Emporium is Australian owned and operated. We are part of the Sydney based SOL Group Australia. After over a decade of experience in the industry, we are proud to be Australia’s leading provider of luxury gift hampers.

We are able to bring you Australia’s leading gift hampers because of the passion and dedication of our team. Everyone who is involved in the hamper creation process, from the amazing producers we partners with to our incredible professional gift wrappers, has an impact on the final product.

Our customers love us
